Ryder

Ryder is a 10-year-old boy and the leader of the PAW Patrol in the children's show Paw Patrol, Ryder runs the Lookout, leads the PAW Patrol, and is the only character to appear in every mission.Ryder is intelligent, creative, and organized. He builds the PAW Patrol's vehicles and equipment, and is skilled at inventing and repairing gadgets.Ryder's vehicle is a high-tech ATV that can transform into three modes:TV, Hovercraft,and Snowmobile.
