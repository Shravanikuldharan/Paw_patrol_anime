Rubble

Rubble, from Paw Patrol, is a construction-themed English Bulldog pup known for his strong, tough exterior with a kind heart, often seen driving a yellow bulldozer and using his digging skills to help the team with building and repair jobs; he's characterized by his catchphrase "Rubble on the double!" and is considered a loyal and reliable member of the PAW Patrol.Not only is he strong and eager to help, he's also funny, cracking a joke whenever the PAW Patrol need a laugh. He loves to skateboard, snowboard, and even DJ.
