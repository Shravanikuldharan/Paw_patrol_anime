Marshal

Marshal is a clumsy, funny and silly Dalmatian puppy and is one of the main pups in PAW Patrol. He has white fur with black spots of random shapes and sizes, he has big floppy ears, blue and black eyes, black sort of heart shaped nose and a happy tail.He is the team's fire pup, as well as the medic pup.His main purpose as a Fire Marshall is to check for fires and extinguish them if necessary, and to use his ladder to rescue animals from high places.
