Chase

Chase is a German Shepherd and the tallest pup in the PAW Patrol. A natural leader, he has a can-do attitude, “by the book” approach, and a blue hat! No matter the mission, he ill sniff out a solution (though he might sneeze around cats and feathers, he is allergic). And when it comes to solving mysteries, he is the best! He is like if Sherlock Holmes was nice. And a dog. Like a nice, Sherlock Holmes dog.
